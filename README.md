# THK-Store-Annual-Data-Analysis-Report-2025-2026-
I recently completed an end-to-end data analysis project on a year-long transactional dataset for a retail store. The goal was to extract actionable insights from raw sales data and present them in a clear, decision-friendly format.
Key Questions Answered
Which customer segment contributes the most to sales?
How do purchasing patterns vary across age groups?
What is the gender-wise distribution of sales?
Which states generate the highest revenue?
How do sales and order volumes trend throughout the year?
What is the status distribution of transactions (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)?
📈 Highlights from the Report
Women contribute 64% of total sales, indicating a dominant customer segment
Adults account for the highest share (~50%) in sales distribution
Top-performing states include Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh
Seasonal trends observed in monthly sales and order volume patterns
Majority of transactions are successfully delivered, with smaller proportions in returns/cancellations
